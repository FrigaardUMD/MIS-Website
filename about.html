My Passions & Interests

Video Production

I started editing videos with free editing tools in middle school becuase I wanted to post comedy videos on YouTube. Even though I never really got that far in my career as a YouTuber, my love for video production stuck with me.

I have experienced relative growth from having to learn how to use different editing programs and tools in my free time, as well as developing the skills required for improving the flow of what I create. It’s been satisfying for me when I add music to a video, lining up the timing of clips, and ultimately watching the final product created.

The experiences that I have encountered through this hobby have increased both my attention to detail and my creativity, it's also given me further insight in my ability to evaluate and examine media in general. I have found myself paying attention more to the various styles and techniques of editing that are utilized in their films, television and videos online. Although I am most definitely an amature, video editing is something that I can see as being part of my life much longer.

Exercising / Lifting

I started lifting weights in high school after I joined the football team. At the time I may have been described as being "skinny fat", and I wanted to get stronger and more muscular to compete with everyone else on the team. I didn't wake up jacked (and I am still not yet, unfortunately), but watching my progressivise improvement over time was increadibly awarding.

I’ve increased my strength and overall health but the most important thing for me was my further development of consistency and discipline. Lifting has reinforced the idea of progress being something that can only be made with time, nothing comes right away. Being able to hit a new personal record every now and then is a great feeling and can only come with commitment. This is something that is seen in many fields and can be applied to any developing skill.

Even if it's not something that will every be directly related to my career, any form of excercise is something that is everyone should be partaking in. Not just for the physical benefits, but also for the mental benefits. Reinforcing the idea of consistency and setting goals for myself is ethic that is increadibly valuable.
